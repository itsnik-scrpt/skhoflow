# SkhoFlow

**SkhoFlow** is a professional productivity suite designed for students, developers, and teams who demand powerful tools without the overhead of managing separate applications across different devices.

The platform delivers a unified workspace — accessible on the web, desktop, and mobile — where writing, presenting, and coding all happen under one roof, with one account, and one consistent experience.

---

## What is SkhoFlow?

Most people juggle three or four different applications just to write, present, and code. SkhoFlow eliminates that fragmentation. Everything lives in one place, synchronized across all your devices, with no installation friction on the web and a native-quality experience on desktop and mobile.

The product is built for people who take their work seriously — professionals who want software that behaves like professional software, not a watered-down browser toy.

---

## Platform Availability

SkhoFlow runs everywhere you work:

- **Web** — fully accessible from any modern browser, no installation required.
- **Desktop** — a native application for Windows, macOS, and Linux, offering deeper system integration, offline support, and a faster, distraction-free environment.
- **Mobile** — a dedicated application for iOS and Android, designed for reviewing, editing on the go, and staying connected to your workspace from any device.

All platforms share the same account, the same files, and the same real-time sync.

---

## The Tools

SkhoFlow launches with three core tools. This is the foundation — many more tools are planned and will be added over time as the platform grows.

### Document Editor
A full-featured word processor built for serious writing. Rich text formatting, heading styles, lists, blockquotes, code blocks, real-time word count, and continuous auto-save. Designed for anything from a short memo to a long technical document, without the visual clutter of traditional office software.

### Slides Editor
A presentation builder with a drag-and-drop canvas. Multi-slide decks, text and shape elements, precise position and size control, per-slide backgrounds, and a clean properties panel. Built for people who want to produce clear, professional presentations without fighting the software.

### Integrated IDE
A complete development environment with a custom-built code editor designed for clarity and speed. Full syntax highlighting across over a dozen languages, a file explorer, multi-file tab management, and an integrated terminal. For developers who want a capable coding environment that loads instantly, without configuring a local setup.

## Who Is It For?

- **Students** who write papers, build projects, and present their work — and want one tool for all of it, on any device.
- **Developers** who need a capable editor accessible from anywhere, without spinning up a local environment.
- **Professionals and teams** who want a clean, focused workspace for documents and presentations, synchronized across desktop and mobile.
- **Anyone** who is tired of switching between multiple applications and losing context every time they change tools.

---

## How It Works

Users create a free account and immediately have access to all current tools across all platforms. Files are stored in the cloud and synchronized in real time. The experience is dark by default, fast, and purpose-built — no ads, no bloat, no onboarding friction.

Paid plans unlock higher storage, unlimited files, export options, offline access, and team collaboration features.

---

## The Design Philosophy

SkhoFlow is built around three principles:

**Clarity.** Every interface decision prioritizes focus. The tools get out of the way so the work can happen.

**Quality.** The editors are not simplified toys. They use the same underlying technology as the software that professionals rely on every day.

**Reach.** Whether you are at a desk, on a laptop, or on a phone, SkhoFlow meets you where you are. One platform, every device.
